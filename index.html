Event features

Things to Do at the Show

Discover boats, explore marine accessories, connect with experts, and enjoy family-friendly activities.

Kids Build-A-Boat Challenge

Little skippers create their own toy boats to take home! Kids build miniature vessels with foam, straws, and tape, then race them in our testing pool.

Captain Q&A Dock

Meet experienced captains for quick tips on docking, navigation, and planning your next adventure on the Finger Lakes.

Sunset Marina Lounge

Recharge in our lounge with lake views, local snacks, and a map wall featuring the best boating routes in the region.

Family Photo Dock

Capture memories at our nautical-themed photo area! Professional backdrop perfect for family photos, boat crew shots, or your first boat purchase celebration.

Wake & Glide Demo Pool

Watch live demonstrations of tow sports gear, paddleboards, and new propulsion tech in our indoor demo pool.

Dockside Music & Eats

Enjoy local food vendors, acoustic sets, and a curated lineup of lake-friendly bites perfect for a day on the show floor.
