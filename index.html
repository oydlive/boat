Things to Do at the Show

Discover boats, explore marine accessories, connect with experts, and enjoy family-friendly activities.

Kids Build-A-Boat Challenge

Little skippers create toy boats to take home, then race them in our testing pool. A hands-on crowd favorite all weekend.

Captain Q&A Dock

Meet experienced captains for quick tips on docking, navigation, and family boating safety.

Boat Parade Showcase

A rotating lineup of new models so guests can hop aboard, tour cabins, and talk directly with dealers.

Family Photo Dock

Capture memories at our nautical-themed photo area! Professional backdrop perfect for family photos, boat crew shots, or your first boat purchase celebration.

Wake & Glide Demo Pool

Live demonstrations of tow sports gear, paddleboards, and easy-to-learn skills for first-time riders.

Dockside Music & Eats

Local food vendors, acoustic sets, and lake-friendly bites keep the crowd fueled throughout the show.
